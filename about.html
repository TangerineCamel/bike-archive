What & why

A partial archive, intentionally.

中文

这是一个持续生长的城市观察档案，记录美团小黄车从第一代至今的结构性演化。 它不设终点，不追求完整，只追求诚实。

选择只记录美团，本身就是一个立场。定义什么是大版本，本身就是一个视角。 这个项目尝试用克制、客观的语言，呈现一个有立场的观察。

所谓版本，并不由年份决定，而由结构性的变化决定: 锁具、条幅材质、前叉形态、 以及车架的根本性重塑。零件页和旁支记录，则是为了让这条主干演化线更清晰。

English

This is an open-ended urban field archive of Meituan yellow bikes, tracing structural evolution from the first generation to the present.

It does not aim to be complete. It aims to be honest. Choosing to document only Meituan is already a position; defining what counts as a major version is already a perspective.

The archive groups bikes by structural change rather than chronology, then uses component studies and branch cases to clarify the main lineage without pretending to be neutral.
